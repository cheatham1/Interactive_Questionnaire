# NHS Continuing Healthcare — Decision Support Tool (interactive questionnaire)

A free, plain-English web app that walks you through the 12 care domains of the NHS Continuing Healthcare **Decision Support Tool (DST)**,
records the level selected for each, and produces:

- an **Assessed Levels of Need** matrix with totals,
- an **indicative conclusion** about likely eligibility, and
- a downloadable **PDF** of the completed form.

Everything runs in the browser. No answers are sent to any server, and the PDF
is generated locally (using a bundled copy of jsPDF).

> **Scope / disclaimer.** This is an unofficial aid based on the July 2022 DST
> care-domain descriptors. It is **not** affiliated with the NHS, does **not**
> replace a statutory assessment, and does **not** decide eligibility.
> Eligibility for CHC is determined by a multidisciplinary team assessing the
> nature, intensity, complexity and unpredictability of a person's needs.
> The indicative conclusion is a simple prompt, not a prediction.
